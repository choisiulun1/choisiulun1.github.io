## Bottom exercise is important for growth
- Bench pressing bring the bar down all the way to your chest
	- Consider using dumbbelss to get deeper
- When doing curls fully extend your arm at the bottom
	- ![[Pasted image 20221227085611.png]]
- When squatting , come down to at least parallel or even further
- <mark style="background: #ABF7F7A6;">Make sure you're actually controlling the weight down </mark>to the bottom position 
	- when you get there , don't cheat or bounce out of it using momentum

## BI-ARTICULATE MUSCLES
- Muscles that cross two joints rather than just one and as a result can be positioned in a way that stretches them even than normal.
	- For this muscles , you make sure that you're doing exercises that puts them into the position of extra stretch

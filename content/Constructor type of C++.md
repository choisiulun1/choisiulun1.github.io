- Constructors that may be called without any argument are [default constructors](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/language/default_constructor "cpp/language/default constructor").
- Constructors that take another object of the same type as the argument are [copy constructors](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/language/copy_constructor "cpp/language/copy constructor") and [move constructors](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/language/move_constructor "cpp/language/move constructor").
